These are fried chicken wings

Chicken wings are very tasty and easy to make, they make you happy and full, which are the best feelings in the world !

Ingredients:
  • Chicken parts like thighs or wings
  • eggs
  • bread crumbs
  • oil (a lot of it)
  • any sauce you prefer
  • flour
  • salt
Steps:
  1. wash your chicken !! and dry it with a clean paper towel;
  2. crack some eggs in a bowl;
  3. put flour and salt in another bowl;
  4. put the bread crumbs in yet another bowl;
  5. dip your chicken in the eggs;
  6. dip your chicken in the flour;
  7. dip your chicken back in the eggs;
  8. dip your chicken in the bread crumbs;

    9. There's a lot of dipping to do.

  9. fry your chicken until golden;
  10. serve with your favourite sauce !
