These are Sushi Rolls

Sushi rolls are the best food i've ever eaten, i would never try to make them myself but i'll love to eat them. They're not hard to make, just need the right technique.

Ingredients:
  • Sea weed
  • rice
  • cheese
  • cucumber
  • fumed salmon
  • caviar
  • shrimp
Steps:
  1. cook your rice;
  2. put a plast of fumed salmon;
  3. put on top a list of sea weed;
  4. spread your rice on the sea weed;
  5. put some sliced cucumber in the middle;
  6. put some shrimps next to it;
  7. roll the thing up however you'll manage and put some caviar on top
  8. et voila ! bon appetit !
